Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a class?

You can book a class by visiting our Classes page, choosing your preferred session, and clicking the “Book Now” button. You will receive a confirmation email immediately after completing your booking.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can. Cancellations and reschedules must be made at least 12 hours before the scheduled class time to receive a full credit or refund. Please log into your account to manage your bookings, or contact us directly for assistance.

Do I need to pay for each class separately?

It depends on your package. We offer both single-class passes and various membership options (monthly or yearly) that allow you to attend multiple classes for one recurring fee. You can view all our pricing options on the Pricing page.

What if I'm new to fitness?

Our studio is welcoming to all levels! We offer beginner-friendly classes clearly marked on the schedule. Our instructors are trained to provide modifications and support, ensuring a safe and comfortable experience for everyone, regardless of their fitness background.
